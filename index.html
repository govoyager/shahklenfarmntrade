Our Story

Shahklen is an agricultural company dedicated to the sustainable production of high-quality vegetables, with a focus on tomatoes and capsicum. With approximately 50 acres of cultivated land, Shahklen leverages modern farming techniques and sustainable practices to meet the growing demand for fresh produce in local and international markets.

Who we are

Shahklen is committed to producing fresh, high-quality vegetables on our farm, cultivating a diverse range of crops, including tomatoes, capsicums, cucumbers, cabbages, and chilies. Our sustainable farming practices ensure that our produce is not only fresh but also environmentally friendly. In addition to our agricultural efforts, we engage in responsible mining activities, contributing to the local economy.
